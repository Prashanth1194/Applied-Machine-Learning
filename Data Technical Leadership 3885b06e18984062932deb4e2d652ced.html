Context

The ‚Äòtechnical leadership‚Äô interview is designed to help us confidently hire IC L50s, by ensuring that we get a signal on some of the key behaviours that set apart our current L50s across data:

Our L50 ICs are leaders, but not people managers. We expect them to set a high technical bar, lead members of their teams towards achieving technical brilliance, and handle high level of ambiguity across a broad scope. L50s should also be impactful both inside and outside their immediate teams. We expect they have previously identified a problem or an opportunity (without it handed to them), and then worked to influence a broad group of stakeholders that there is value in tackling the problem. Then, they execute, leading the delivery of a solution.

Criteria

  • Setting a high technical bar
  • Ability to handle ambiguity and a broad scope
  • Ability to self reflect
  • Proactivity
  • Impacting others

Introduction

The purpose of this interview is for us to understand how well you would perform the Level 50 (which is a lead role at Monzo) and above framework behaviours (point to progression.monzo.com).

We will use behavioural questions to explore your experience and thought process in two areas, aiming to dig deep into one or two topics in each area:
- Comms & Leadership: How do you support, motivate, and impact the people around you?
- Working at Monzo: How will you adapt to apply these skills in a new environment at Monzo?

We'll ask you questions and reflect on situations you've been through that demonstrate related to these behaviours in the framework.

We have a lot to get through, so apologies if we cut you off but we want to try to stick to time. We'll also try to leave 5-10 mins at the end for any questions you may have.

  • Can you tell me a bit more about your current team setup? [Framing question - make the candidate feel comfortable. No more than 5 minutes]
